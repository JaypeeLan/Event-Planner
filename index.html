About Us

For your event, everything matters

Minds & Event unlike any other, gives an unforgettable experience, while making your event stress free. With our experienced team of planners, who specialize in creating unique and memorable moments that will leave a long lasting impression on you and your guests.
With us, ‘magical’ is possible. We will help you create a customed event to your very imagination, while working with the best team in Nigeria, to bring it to reality, from the best vendors to breathe taking designs and to beautiful environments.

Established in 2017, Minds & Event was created to make fine events to the custom taste of our clients. Our goal is to bring magic to reality, while providing the best services and amazing customer services.
